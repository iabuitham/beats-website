Who we are

About the Company

Established in 2004, Beats Events is a premier event management company based in Jordan, extending its roots from EVENTOS in Doha (founded in 2001). The company is led by founder Mr. Jalal Halaweh, with 22 years of expertise in event and venue management, logistics, production, and marketing.

Our Mission

We don’t just organize events—we create unforgettable experiences through deep understanding of client needs, attention to detail, and flawless execution that ensures a safe and enjoyable atmosphere for everyone.

Why We Stand Out

Extensive experience across production and brand management, a detail-driven approach, and an end-to-end system that leaves nothing to chance—from budgeting and logistics to marketing and management.

Regional Strength
